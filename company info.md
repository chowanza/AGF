Transportation can be considered as one of the most important and rising industry in the United States. Trucks in North America
and South America are responsible for the majority of freight movement over land. Also, they are a key piece in the manufacturing
processes and warehousing industries. Trucks serves in the economy by serving as a medium of transportation for raw material, works in
process, and finished goods.
Trucks can be found in different industries. These are able to become in a special equipment that provide an specific service in a
process. For example, in the construction industry, dump trucks and portable concrete mixers move the large amounts of rocks, dirt,
concrete, and other building materials. In Oil and Gas, Trucks are able to carry wirelines, winches, vacuums that provide support to regular
operations.
